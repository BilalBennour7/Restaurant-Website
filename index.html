Our Story

About Bella Napoli

Three generations of passion, tradition, and the finest pizza.

From Naples to New York

In 1987, Nonna Maria Rossi brought her family's treasured pizza recipes from the heart of Naples to the streets of New York City. What began as a tiny storefront with a single wood-fired oven quickly became a beloved neighborhood gem.

Today, her grandson Marco carries on the tradition, hand-stretching every dough, sourcing the finest San Marzano tomatoes and fresh mozzarella, and firing each pizza in our original brick oven at 900°F, just as Nonna taught him.

At Bella Napoli, great pizza is more than food; it's a gathering place, a celebration of community, and a taste of la dolce vita right here in the city.

1

Authentic Recipes

Our recipes have been passed down through three generations, unchanged and perfected over time. Every bite takes you straight to Naples.

2

Finest Ingredients

We import our flour from Italy, use only San Marzano DOP tomatoes, and our mozzarella is made fresh daily. No shortcuts, no compromises.

3

Community First

We're not just a restaurant, we're a family. From charity events to sponsoring local sports teams, giving back is in our DNA.
